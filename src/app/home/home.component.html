Welcome to Hivemind!

Hivemind is a social media web-app where you can share your ideas with other users and find out what they think

You can view other people's ideas from the main feed, or submit your own!

Once an idea is submitted, other people can vote on it, and decide whether they agree with it or not

You can also comment on people's ideas.

If you want to spice up the look of your posts, try using Markdown!
